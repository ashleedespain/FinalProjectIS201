Quick and Easy Travel Ideas

St. George, Utah

  1. Visit one of many National Parks; Zion, Arches, Bryce Canyon, Canyonlands, Bears Ears, Capitol Reef
  2. Stay more local and visit Snow Canyon State Park or Sand Hollow Reservoir
  3. Watch a play at Tuacahn Ampitheater Center for the Arts


New Orleans, Louisiana

  1. Tour the Bayou on a swamp tour
  2. Go to a Mardi Gras parade (in February)
  3. Try alligator, gumbo, and jambalaya


San Antonio, Texas

  1. Fill up gas and get food at Buc-ees
  2. Visit the Riverwalk in downgtown San Antonio
  3. Try some delicious Tex-Mex or BBQ
